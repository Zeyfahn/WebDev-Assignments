How to bake a Cake

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Refined flour
  • Milk
  • Cherries
  • Chocochips
  • Chocolate Syrup

Instructions

  1. Mix the egg white and milk and the refined flour
  2. add cocoa as per your need
  3. create a batter
  4. store it in a cake tub
  5. leave it in the oven for 30 minutes
  6. decorate with chocochips and chocolate syrup
And now your delicious chocolate cake is ready!
